About Us

Vinberly Micro Credit is a leading microfinance institution dedicated to empowering individuals and small businesses across Ghana. Our mission is to provide accessible financial solutions that enable our clients to achieve their dreams, improve their livelihoods, and contribute to the economic development of their communities.

We are committed to fostering financial inclusion and sustainable growth in Ghana, West Africa, and the wider African continent. Our tailored credit facilities are designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients, helping them build a brighter future.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for a loan?

Requirements vary by loan type but generally include valid ID, business registration (for business loans), proof of income, and a guarantor. Please contact us or refer to the "Apply Now" section for detailed information.

How long does it take to process a loan?

Loan processing typically takes between 24 to 72 hours, depending on the completeness of your application and verification processes.

What are your interest rates?

Our interest rates are competitive and depend on the loan amount, tenor, and type of loan. Please discuss with our loan officers for personalized information.
